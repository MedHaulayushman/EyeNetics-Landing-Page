How Our Model Works

Next Gen Data Analytics

The working of our system is fairly simple: all you need to do is input some patient data and the retinal scan. The uploaded retinal image is then processed by our ML Model, which classifies the input retinal image based on patterns of the blood vessels. We are currently using a dataset of 32,000 retinal images to train our model.
